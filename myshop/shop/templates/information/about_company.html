About our company

Pets Love is a leading pet store dedicated to providing exceptional products and services for pets and their owners.

Founded in 2005 by John and Sarah Smith, Pets Love has grown to become a trusted name in the pet industry, with multiple locations across the United States.

At Pets Love, we believe that pets are family, and we strive to offer a wide range of high-quality pet care products, including food, toys, accessories, and grooming supplies.

Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is passionate about ensuring the well-being and happiness of pets, and we provide personalized assistance and expert advice to our valued customers.

In addition to our comprehensive product selection, we offer various services such as professional grooming, pet training, and adoption programs to help pets and their owners thrive.

At Pets Love, we are committed to fostering the bond between pets and their owners through exceptional products, compassionate care, and a dedication to customer satisfaction.

Visit one of our locations today and experience the love we have for pets!

Company History

Establishment of Pets Love

March 15, 2005

Pets Love was founded by John and Sarah Smith in the bustling city of New York.

With a passion for animals and a vision to provide exceptional pet care products and services, John and Sarah set out to create a welcoming and trusted pet store.

Their dedication to customer satisfaction and the well-being of pets quickly gained recognition, laying a strong foundation for the future growth of Pets Love.

Expansion to San Francisco

September 21, 2007

Pets Love expanded its operations and opened a new branch in the beautiful city of San Francisco, California.

The San Francisco location quickly became a hub for pet lovers, offering a wide range of high-quality pet food, toys, accessories, and professional grooming services.

The warm and friendly atmosphere of the store, combined with the expertise of the dedicated staff, made Pets Love a beloved destination for pet owners in the Bay Area.

Grand Opening in Miami

June 8, 2010

Pets Love celebrated the grand opening of its Miami location, bringing joy to pet lovers in the vibrant city.

The Miami store showcased a wide variety of pets for adoption, including dogs, cats, birds, and small animals, providing a loving home for many furry friends.

Customers in Miami also had access to expert pet training sessions and educational workshops organized by Pets Love to promote responsible pet ownership.

10-Year Anniversary

November 30, 2015

Pets Love proudly commemorated its 10-year anniversary, expressing gratitude to its loyal customers for their unwavering support over the years.

To mark this milestone, Pets Love organized a series of special events, including adoption drives, charity fundraisers for animal welfare organizations, and discounted offers on a wide range of pet products.

The 10-year anniversary celebration served as a testament to the strong bond between Pets Love and the pet-loving community it served.

Expansion to Chicago

February 14, 2020

Pets Love expanded once again, opening a new store in the lively city of Chicago, Illinois.

The Chicago location featured state-of-the-art pet care facilities, including a dedicated grooming salon, a pet daycare center, and a training area.

With its commitment to excellence and personalized customer service, Pets Love quickly became a trusted name in the pet industry within the Chicago community.
